Privacy Statement

How BHD Asia collects, uses, and protects your personal data in line with Singapore's PDPA.

Contact Us ← Back to Home

Last updated: June 2026  |  Business & Human Development Consulting Pte Ltd

Introduction

Business and Human Development Consulting Pte Ltd ("BHD Asia", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 of Singapore (PDPA). This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you engage with our services, visit our website, or attend our events and programmes.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identification information: name, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact information: email address, telephone number, mailing address
  • Professional information: job title, company name, industry, years of experience
  • Financial information: billing details for the purpose of processing payments for services and events (we do not store full payment card details)
  • Communications: messages you send us via our contact form, email, or during coaching and consulting sessions
  • Event registration data: dietary preferences, emergency contact details for in-person events
  • Website usage data: cookies, IP addresses, browser type, pages visited (collected via analytics tools)

2. Purposes of Collection and Use

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide coaching, consulting, training, and facilitation services to you
  • To process event registrations and payments
  • To communicate with you about your enquiries, bookings, and service updates
  • To send newsletters, event announcements, and relevant insights (where you have consented)
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To improve our website, services, and programmes
  • For internal administrative and record-keeping purposes

3. Consent

By providing your personal data to us, or by continuing to use our services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Statement. Where required by law, we will obtain your explicit consent before using your data for specific purposes such as marketing communications.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at isabelle@bhdasia.com. Please note that withdrawal of consent may affect our ability to provide certain services to you.

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • To trusted service providers who assist in our business operations (e.g., payment processors, IT support) under strict confidentiality obligations
  • To our associate coaches and facilitators, where necessary to deliver the services you have engaged
  • To regulatory authorities or law enforcement agencies where required by law
  • In the event of a business merger, acquisition, or restructuring, where personal data may be transferred to the successor entity

5. Data Accuracy

We take reasonable steps to ensure the personal data we hold is accurate and up to date. You are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of the information you provide us. Please contact us promptly if your personal data changes or if you believe we hold inaccurate information about you.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations. When personal data is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or anonymise it securely.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include secure data storage, access controls, and confidentiality agreements with staff and associates. However, no transmission of data over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data transmitted to our website.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytics data. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, this may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

9. Access and Correction

Under the PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate personal data
  • Withdraw consent to the use of your personal data for specific purposes

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at isabelle@bhdasia.com. We will respond to requests within 30 days.

10. Cross-Border Transfers

Where it is necessary to transfer your personal data outside Singapore (for example, to associate coaches based in other countries), we will ensure that such transfers comply with the PDPA and that appropriate protections are in place.

11. Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated statement on our website with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this statement periodically.
