ICF — International Coaching Federation

Certified Member

SIMI is Singapore's national body for mediator accreditation. Isabelle is a SIMI-accredited mediator, enabling her to provide professional conflict resolution and mediation services alongside her coaching practice.

About SIMI

Brainz Magazine
