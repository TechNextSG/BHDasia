TRE® (Tension & Trauma Releasing Exercises) is a series of neurogenic exercises that help the body release deep patterns of muscular tension and stress. Developed by Dr. David Berceli, TRE® supports recovery from stress, overwhelm, and long-held physical tension. It is safe for most individuals and can be practised independently once learned.
