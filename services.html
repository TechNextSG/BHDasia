Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with SMEs or only large corporations?

We work with organisations of all sizes — from growth-stage companies to global multinationals. Our programmes are co-designed to fit your team size, budget and goals.

How long does a typical engagement take?

Engagements range from a single leadership workshop to a 12-month executive coaching programme. We start with a discovery conversation to understand your needs and propose a timeline.

Can you deliver programmes outside Singapore?

Yes. BHD Asia has delivered programmes across Asia Pacific, Japan, Europe and the Middle East. We adapt delivery language and format to your team's location and culture.

Is coaching available in languages other than English?

Isabelle coaches in English, French and Portuguese, allowing BHD Asia to serve multinational leadership teams across different regions.

What is the first step to working with BHD Asia?

Book a free 30-minute discovery call. We will listen to your challenges and — only if there is a genuine fit — propose a programme design.
