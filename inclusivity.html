Our Commitment to Inclusion

At Business and Human Development Consulting Pte Ltd (BHD Asia), inclusion is not an aspiration — it is a core value and an active practice. We believe that every human being deserves to be seen, heard, and valued for exactly who they are. Our work is grounded in deep respect for diversity in all its forms, and we are committed to creating spaces where every person can show up fully and authentically.

This statement outlines our six core commitment areas and the actions we take to live these values in everything we do.

?

1. Dignity and Respect for Every Person

We are unconditionally committed to treating every individual with dignity, respect, and care — regardless of race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, age, disability, socioeconomic status, educational background, or any other characteristic. In all our interactions — coaching, facilitation, events, and partnerships — we hold this as non-negotiable.

?

2. Diversity as a Strength

We celebrate diversity of thought, background, culture, and experience as one of the greatest drivers of creativity, resilience, and growth. Our founding philosophy — drawing on the linguistic, cultural, generational, and professional diversity of our team and network — means that inclusion is not an add-on; it is the foundation of how we create value. We actively seek and welcome diverse perspectives in all our work.

?

3. Psychologically Safe Spaces

We are committed to creating psychologically safe environments in all our programmes, workshops, and events. This means:

  • Zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or discriminatory language or behaviour
  • Active facilitation of inclusive group dynamics where all voices are welcomed
  • Clear processes for raising concerns or incidents
  • Consistent role-modelling of respectful, empathetic communication by all BHD Asia representatives
?

4. Accessible and Equitable Services

We strive to make our services as accessible as possible. We offer online and in-person options to reduce geographical barriers, provide flexible scheduling where feasible, and are open to discussions about adjusted pricing for individuals or organisations facing financial constraints. We are committed to designing programmes that are culturally sensitive and do not privilege one cultural framework over another.

?

5. Inclusive Partnerships and Referrals

We apply the same inclusivity standards to our selection of partners, associate coaches, and referral networks. We actively seek to work with professionals from diverse backgrounds and to expand our network beyond the dominant cultural and geographical narratives in our field. We believe that a diverse ecosystem of practice produces better outcomes for everyone.

?

6. Ongoing Learning and Accountability

Inclusion is a practice, not a destination. We are committed to:

  • Continuously educating ourselves about systemic bias, privilege, and inclusive practice
  • Actively seeking feedback from clients and partners about our inclusivity in practice
  • Reviewing and updating our policies and practices regularly
  • Being accountable when we fall short, and using those moments as opportunities to learn and improve

We invite our clients, partners, and communities to hold us to these commitments and to share feedback — positive or challenging — at isabelle@bhdasia.com.

At BHD Asia, we believe that when we create truly inclusive spaces — in organisations, in communities, and within ourselves — we unlock the full potential of every human being. That is the heart of our mission: to Highlight, Enhance, and Appreciate the Human in Business.
