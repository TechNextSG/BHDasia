Simba Stenqvist

Somatic Practitioner & TRE® Specialist · BHD Asia

"The nervous system is the foundation for clarity, presence, and steady decision-making under pressure — not a technique layered on top of a person's life."

Part of the BHD Asia team, Simba brings more than twenty-five years of hands-on practice in bodywork, nervous-system regulation, and energetic healing into one integrated approach — developed in close collaboration with Dr. David Berceli, founder of TRE® (Tension & Trauma Release Exercises).

His work is grounded in anatomy, physiology, and nervous-system science, with each session shaped around what the body in front of him actually needs. Simba teaches and delivers this work across Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand and internationally — supporting practitioners, leaders, and high-performing individuals who are technically excellent yet privately sense a ceiling that more knowledge hasn't been able to move.
