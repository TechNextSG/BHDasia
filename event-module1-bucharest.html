The Training

What Is TRE® Module 1?

TRE® (Tension, Stress & Trauma Releasing Exercises) is a simple yet powerful body-based tool, developed by Dr. David Berceli, that activates a natural neurogenic tremoring mechanism to help discharge accumulated stress, tension and trauma from the nervous system.

Module 1 is the foundational certification level, giving you an in-depth theoretical understanding and supervised hands-on experience of TRE® — equipping you to integrate neurogenic tremoring safely into your professional practice and personal self-care.

This is the first time TRE® Module 1 is offered in Europe in English, opening the certification pathway to international coaches, therapists, and healthcare professionals based in or visiting Romania.

Who Should Attend

  • Executive, life and business coaches seeking somatic tools
  • Therapists, counsellors and psychologists
  • Healthcare professionals, nurses and allied health practitioners
  • Yoga, Pilates and movement teachers and trainers
  • HR professionals and organisational wellbeing leads
  • Anyone working with stress, trauma and resilience professionally
