The Workshop

Self-Care & Presence for Coaches

This 2-day in-person workshop is designed specifically for coaches — whether in training or at ACC, PCC or MCC level — who want to develop embodied self-care practices, prevent burnout and compassion fatigue, and deepen their somatic presence with clients.

TRE® (Tension, Stress & Trauma Releasing Exercises) activates the body's natural neurogenic tremoring response — a reflex that discharges stored stress and tension from the nervous system, builds resilience and promotes deep regulation.

This workshop counts as Module 1 of the Global TRE® Provider Certification Program — your first step toward becoming a Certified TRE® Provider.

This workshop is for you if you are:

  • Coaches in training (ACC, PCC & MCC level)
  • Looking to prevent & recover from compassion fatigue and burnout
  • Wanting a body-led modality for deep nervous system resilience
  • Seeking to complement your coaching certifications with TRE®
  • Considering the full Global TRE® Provider Certification (Modules 1, 2 & 3)
