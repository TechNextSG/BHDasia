About TRE®

Why TRE® Is Worth Certifying In

TRE® (Tension, Stress & Trauma Releasing Exercises) activates the body's natural neurogenic tremoring response — a reflex that releases stored stress and trauma from the nervous system. Developed by Dr. David Berceli and backed by 40+ years of research, it is used with PTSD, chronic stress and general wellness populations across 100+ countries.

Unlike many modalities, TRE® can be taught to clients for independent practice — making it a high-value, scalable addition to any practitioner's toolkit.

This certification is for:

  • Coaches (executive, life, wellness) wanting somatic tools
  • Therapists, counsellors and psychologists seeking body-based approaches
  • HR and OD professionals in corporate wellness and resilience
  • Physiotherapists, yoga teachers and movement practitioners
  • Anyone working in trauma support, conflict resolution or post-crisis recovery
  • Practitioners who have experienced TRE® personally and want to share it
The Certification Path

How the Certification Works

Three modules — each a 2-day workshop plus online sessions — take you from personal practice to Certified Global TRE® Provider.

1
Module 1 — Your Personal TRE® Practice  ·  29–30 Aug 2026

2-day workshop + 4 online personal TRE® sessions. Explore the stress response, understand the therapeutic tremor response, and experience TRE® as a self-practice — including self-regulation, containment and grounding strategies. The first step for anyone keen to pursue the certification path. 21 ICF CCEUs included.

2
Module 2 — Teaching TRE® to One Person  ·  26–27 Sep 2026

2-day workshop + 4 online supervision sessions. Open to those who have completed Module 1. Deepens Module 1 content, covers Polyvagal theory and basic TRE® interventions, with hands-on practice teaching TRE® one-to-one.

3
Module 3 — Teaching TRE® to Groups  ·  13–14 Feb 2027

2-day workshop + 4 online supervision sessions. Open to those who have completed all Module 2 requirements. Covers advanced TRE® interventions, the role of fascia, transference and counter-transference, and experience leading groups of 3+ persons.

✓
Global TRE® Certified Provider

On completing all three modules you receive your Global TRE® Provider certificate — qualified to offer TRE® to the public in individual and group settings, and to competently guide clients through the TRE® process.
