Our Story

BHD Asia leverages language, cultural, generational and professional diversity as well as the unique experiences of the personal journeys

Established by Isabelle Claus Teixeira and Rouviere Teixeira in Singapore in 2012, Business and Human Development Consulting partners with clients across the world.

Business and Human Development Pte Ltd is a Leadership Coaching and Leadership Development boutique company, and also offers Language consulting and teaching in French.

We work in close collaboration with our clients and offer tailored-made co-designed solutions, and not "off the shelf" training programs. Diagnostic is always part of our work.

Some of our clients companies to date: ByteDance (TikTok), NTT DATA, Novartis Pharmaceuticals Japan, Novartis Pharmaceuticals Russia, Zimmer Biomet Asia Pacific, Philipps, VIFOR Pharma Asia Pacific, Fresenius Medical Care Asia Pacific, GILEAD Russia, Fresenius Medical Care Singapore, ALCON Middle East and Eastern Europe, ALCON US, ALCON Dubai, Luxmi Teas, Heineken Asia Pacific, DSM, Levi Strauss Japan, Roquette Asia Pacific, RECKITT, Obeetee, HST Medical Pte Ltd, Robert Walters Singapore, Robert Walters Thailand and Vietnam, VISA, Mastercard Pte Ltd, Viva Foundation, Eton House International school, UWC SEA, etc…

ISABELLE CLAUS TEIXEIRA

Co-Founder and Director

Isabelle has over 27 years of experience in Senior HR Business partnering and regional Talent Management and Organisational Effectiveness experiences across Asia Pacific, Japan, Russia, US and Europe and is also a certified Leadership coach since 2012.

Bringing out the best in each of us

Mission

We will be recognised as an international, competent, professional and caring business partner by our clients and associates.

Our mission is to co-create business and HR bespoke solutions relevant and impactful for our clients, through a collaborative consulting and delivery process.

We partner and demand from our associates entrepreneurial attitudes and mindset, high personal accountability, passion and commitment to deliver true add-value to our clients versus personal high financial gains.

We help each of our associates make their professional and personal goals a reality. Through welcoming diverse profiles, embrace flexible, virtual and borderless working practices.

With our partners and clients, we role model collaboration versus competition.

Values & Beliefs

♡

Human, not resources

We are passionate about contributing to developing leaders, management practices and organisation cultures that are focused on deep respect and deep appreciation for the human beings we are. We put respect, empathy, psychological safety and courage at the centre of our recommendations and designs.

☍

True win-win relationships

We love working collaboratively and transparently hand-in-hand with our clients. We are passionate about transferring our knowledge to ensure our clients remain autonomous and independent. We respect our clients roles and visions. We believe repeat business will come when we have set up our clients for success.

✦

Diversity as a passion and a way of life

We are passionate about diversity of thoughts, diversity of approaches and methodologies. We ground all we do in diversity. As far as we are concerned, it is the only way to delivering the best results to you.

⊕

Integrity, ethical behavior and transparency

With all communications and decisions, relationships and work practices.
