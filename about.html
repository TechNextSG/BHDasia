Our Story

Built on Diversity.
Driven by People.

BHD Asia leverages language, cultural, generational and professional diversity — along with the unique experiences of our team's personal journeys — to deliver impactful, tailored solutions for clients around the world.

Established by Isabelle Claus Teixeira and Rouviere Teixeira in Singapore in 2012, Business and Human Development Consulting partners with clients across the globe.

We are a boutique Leadership Coaching and Leadership Development consultancy, also offering French language consulting and teaching. Our work spans Asia Pacific, Japan, Russia, Europe, and the US.

We work in close collaboration with our clients, offering co-designed, tailored solutions — never off-the-shelf programmes. Diagnostics are always part of our process.
