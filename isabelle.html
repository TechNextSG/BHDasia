Isabelle Claus Teixeira

Leadership Coach & Talent Development Specialist · Co-Founder & Director, BHD Asia

"Helping You Find & Forge Your Own Path"

Isabelle brings 27+ years of Senior HR Business Partnering, Regional Talent Management, and Organisational Effectiveness experience across Asia Pacific, Japan, Russia, the US and Europe. Since 1996, she has held global, regional and country HR roles across Automotive, Software, IT, Pharmaceuticals, Medical Devices, and more.

Having lived and worked in 9 countries — France, UK, Germany, Spain, Portugal, Belgium, Switzerland, Singapore, and Japan — she connects authentically with clients navigating the human complexities of international business.

Coaching Specialisations

  • Executive Coaching
  • Transition Coaching (role changes & new leader onboarding)
  • Performance Coaching
  • Career Coaching
  • Self Awareness Development
  • 360° Debrief & Multi-Perspective Feedback
  • Somatic Coaching (body-based work integrated with neuroscience)
  • TRE® Provider & Trainer Trainee (Tension & Trauma Releasing Exercises)

Credentials & Certifications

  • Certified Leadership Coach since 2012
  • 1,300+ paid coaching hours delivered
  • Accredited Mediator — Singapore International Mediation Institute (SIMI)
  • Forbes Coaches Council Executive Contributor
  • Brainz Magazine Contributor (September 2023)
  • Accredited in Global Cultural Framework, Stakeholder-Centred Coaching, Neuro Leadership, Somatic Coaching, and Team Coaching

Global Perspective

Fluent in French and English (conversational Portuguese), Isabelle's multicultural background lets her engage meaningfully across cultures and industries. Her career spans Automotive, Software, IT, Clinical Research, Trading, Shipping, Semiconductors, Pharmaceuticals, and Medical Devices.

Working with Isabelle

Isabelle works with senior executives, emerging leaders, and entrepreneurs ready to create meaningful change. Every engagement starts with a diagnostic conversation — no off-the-shelf solutions. Whether navigating leadership transition, burnout, or a major life pivot, she brings rigour, warmth, and deep respect for who you are.
