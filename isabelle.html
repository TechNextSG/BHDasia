Isabelle Claus Teixeira

Co-Founder & Director — Business and Human Development Consulting Pte Ltd

With 27+ years in Senior HR Business Partnering, Regional Talent Management, and Organisational Effectiveness across Asia Pacific, Japan, Russia, the US and Europe, Isabelle brings a rare breadth of experience to every engagement. Having lived and worked in 9 countries, she understands the human complexity behind international business firsthand.

Coaching Specialisations

  • Executive & Leadership Coaching
  • Career Transition & Life Coaching
  • Resilience & Stress Management Coaching
  • Intercultural & Cross-Cultural Coaching
  • Conflict Management & Mediation
  • TRE® Certified Provider (Tension & Trauma Releasing Exercises)

Credentials

  • ICF Certified Coach — International Coaching Federation
  • Certified Leadership Coach since 2012
  • Accredited Mediator — Singapore International Mediation Institute (SIMI)
  • Forbes Coaches Council Member & Contributor
  • 1,300+ paid coaching hours delivered
  • Certified in multiple psychometric and assessment tools

Global Perspective

Fluent in French and English (conversational Portuguese), Isabelle's multicultural background enables her to connect authentically with clients across cultures and industries — from Automotive and Pharma to Technology, FMCG, and Financial Services.

Working with Isabelle

Isabelle works with senior executives, emerging leaders, and entrepreneurs ready to create meaningful change. Her approach is always diagnostic and collaborative — no off-the-shelf solutions. Whether navigating leadership transition, burnout, or a major life pivot, she brings warmth, rigour, and deep respect for who you are.
